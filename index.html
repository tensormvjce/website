Welcome to Tensor

An enthusiastic and vibrant AI/ML community at MVJ College of Engineering

Vision

We work towards an empowering future where AI and ML transform lives, industries, and societies through inclusive innovation, education, and collaboration fostering growth and development. Tensor strives to shape the AI future, exchange ideas, and propel innovation as a team.

Mission

To foster a vibrant community of AIML enthusiasts, professionals, and researchers, and develop practical solutions and frameworks to tackle real-world challenges.

About Tensor

Tensor is an enthusiastic and vibrant community-driven AI and ML club at MVJ College of Engineering. The club fosters collaboration, project development, knowledge sharing, and networking opportunities through workshops, hackathons, and seminars. As machines awaken, humanity evolves. Tensor’s primary focus is to provide a platform for students to explore and deepen their understanding of cutting-edge technologies and concepts like data science, neural networks, and computational models. The sonata of human evolution and machine brilliance, empowering the excellence of AI one tensor at a time!
